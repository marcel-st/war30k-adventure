# Death Guard 30K Art Bible (TPS Vertical Slice)

This project targets a Death Guard loyalist marine presentation aligned with the Great Crusade / Horus Heresy era.

## Character Pillars

- Heavy military silhouette: broad pauldrons, reinforced greaves, large backpack mass.
- Armor palette: ash/off-white ceramite with muted legion green accents.
- Surface treatment: chipped edges, grime accumulation, soot streaking, oxidized trim.
- Keep contrast practical and grounded: avoid cartoon saturation.

## Environment Pillars

- Trench-gothic ruins with industrial details and monumental scale cues.
- Materials favor rough, weathered metals and scarred stone.
- Fog and dust should preserve readability while adding battlefield depth.

## Lighting Targets

- Cool low-key ambient base.
- Strong directional moon/ash light.
- Sparse toxic green practical accent lights for mood and objective guidance.

## Material Rules

- Ceramite roughness: generally high (0.65-0.9), with edge wear exposing lower roughness.
- Metals: aged brass/iron with limited specular hotspots.
- Decals: subtle legion markings, tactical hazard chevrons, and damage scars.

## VFX Guidance

- Bolt weapon fire: dense muzzle flash, short hot tracer line, metallic impact sparks.
- Atmospheric effects: drifting embers, ash particles, low smoke volumes.

## Current Vertical Slice Status

- Placeholder primitive meshes are used for the marine and world.
- Palette already follows the target direction.
- Next content pass should replace primitives with blockout assets while preserving this visual language.
