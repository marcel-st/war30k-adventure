# Death Guard 30K Art Bible (TPS Vertical Slice)

This project targets a loyalist Death Guard presentation aligned with the Great Crusade / Horus Heresy era, while preserving gameplay readability for a fast-moving third-person shooter loop.

## Character Direction

- Heavy military silhouette: broad pauldrons, reinforced greaves, backpack mass.
- Armor palette: ash/off-white ceramite with muted legion green panels.
- Surface treatment: chipped edges, grime buildup, soot streaking, oxidized trim.
- Posture and motion should feel deliberate and weighty rather than agile.

## Enemy and Encounter Readability

- Enemy archetypes should remain visually distinct at medium range:
  - traitor melee silhouette reads as close-range threat.
  - cultist ranged silhouette reads as suppressive fire source.
  - elite/boss silhouettes read as priority targets.
- Attack telegraphs (especially boss strike/nova windows) must stay high-contrast against floor surfaces.
- VFX color selection should support telegraph readability before style.

## Environment Direction

- Trench-gothic ruins with industrial details and monumental scale cues.
- Materials favor rough, weathered metals and scarred stone.
- Fog and dust should add depth but not obscure incoming fire vectors.
- Mission lanes should keep clear contrast between playable path, cover, and hazard zones.

## Lighting Targets

- Cool low-key ambient base.
- Strong directional ash/moon-style key light.
- Sparse toxic-green practical accents for objective guidance and ability hazard readability.

## Material Rules

- Ceramite roughness generally high (0.65-0.9), with edge wear exposing lower roughness.
- Metals lean aged brass/iron with limited specular hotspots.
- Decals remain restrained: legion marks, hazard chevrons, and battle damage.

## VFX and Audio-Coupled Style

- Boltgun fire should pair visual recoil cues with hard metallic SFX impact signatures.
- Boss telegraphs should have both visual and audio cue reinforcement.
- Ability visuals should map to mechanical effect:
  - resilience surge: defensive readability.
  - toxic grenade/hazard: persistent area-denial readability.
  - rally command: mobility/tempo readability.

## Current Vertical Slice Status

- Placeholder/blockout geometry is still used in major scene areas.
- Palette and atmosphere already follow the intended 30K direction.
- Audio identity is now integrated (combat hard rock, intermission ballad, reactive SFX).
- Next art pass should replace placeholder geometry while preserving established readability rules.
